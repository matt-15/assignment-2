The eclectic powerbank is a slim and light powerbank, perfect for carrying around. Able to charge your devices quickly using Qualcomm Quick Charge 3.0 technology.
It has dual output USB ports for charging your devices, and a input USB-C port used to charge up the powerbank. This powerbank has a 20000mAh battery to keep all your other
devices fully charged.
It comes with a 1 year warranty.
