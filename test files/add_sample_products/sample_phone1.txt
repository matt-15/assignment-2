F9 has a 6.5" screen display with a nearly bezel-less Infinity display gives an immerse, cinematic quality to whatever you are viewing.
It supports 40W fast-charging and with a 4000mAh battery, guarantee to last you the entire day. It also has 8GB RAM and 1TB upgradable internal storage.
